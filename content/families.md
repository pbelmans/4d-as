The known families of four-dimensional Artin–Schelter regular algebras. The
presentations are largely **quadratic**: each algebra is a quotient of the free
algebra on four generators \(x_1, x_2, x_3, x_4\) by quadratic relations, the
relations below being understood to equal \(0\).
