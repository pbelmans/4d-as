This site collects what is known about
**quadratic four-dimensional Artin–Schelter regular algebras**,
giving the noncommutative analogues of \(\mathbb{P}^3\).

There are by now many such families, scattered across the literature: the
elliptic Sklyanin algebra and its cocycle twists, graded (skew) Clifford
algebras, the quadric-and-line algebras of Vancliff, the quantizations of
Poisson structures coming from Pym's work, the additive family of
Lecoutre–Sierra, and a long list of iterated Ore extensions. The recent
classification effort of [arXiv:2511.08390](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.08390)
served as the starting point for this website.

Everything is generated from [small data files](https://github.com/pbelmans/4d-as/tree/main/data).
Corrections and additions are very welcome — please open an issue on
[the repository](https://github.com/pbelmans/4d-as).

For now, this is still a _draft_, and I will not announce its existence publicly just yet.
If you have found this website already, congratulations!
It might look different soon.
