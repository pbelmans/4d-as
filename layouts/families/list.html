Deformations of the polynomial algebra: Pym's six cases

The commutative polynomial ring, together with the six irreducible components of the variety of quantum deformations of \(\mathbb{P}^3\) classified by Pym (arXiv:1403.6444): the skew polynomial ring \(L(1,1,1,1)\), the Sklyanin algebra \(R(2,2)\) and its central extension \(R(1,3)\), the Cassidy–Goetz–Shelton algebra \(L(1,1,2)\), the Ore extension \(S(2,3)\), and the exceptional component \(E(3)\).
